Guide

  • Default outer boundary is no-flow unless you draw EP/phreatic lines.
  • EP lines impose fixed hydraulic head. Datum is always taken to be the bottom of the domain.
  • Phreatic line is a user-drawn fixed head equal to elevation.
  • No-flow lines and no-flow polygons act as impermeable boundaries.
  • Canvas controls: +/- zoom, Pan drag, Fit reset view.
  • Keyboard: Esc cancels drawing, Delete removes selected item.
  • Line/polygon edit shortcuts in Select: choose an item first, then Alt+edge click adds vertex, Ctrl/Cmd+vertex click deletes.
